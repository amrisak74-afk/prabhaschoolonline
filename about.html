About Our School

Rooted in values, focused on every learner.

Prabha Gyan Jyoti Public School, Gangoh has supported children from Pre Nursery to Class VIII since 2011 — with care, discipline, creativity, and strong academic foundations.

Our Identity

A warm school community with a clear learning purpose.

Since 2011, the school has worked to build confidence, respect, communication, and curiosity in every child. Students learn through classroom teaching, activities, celebrations, sports, and regular guidance from their teachers.
