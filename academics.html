Academics

Strong basics from Pre Nursery to Class VIII.

Our academic programme focuses on reading, writing, numeracy, communication, confidence, and regular progress tracking for every child.

Our Approach

Structured learning with measurable progress.

01 Concept-based classroom teaching: topics explained through examples, board work, reading, discussion, and regular revision so students understand before memorizing.
02 Tests and feedback: notebooks, oral work, class tests, corrections, and teacher remarks help parents and students track progress clearly.
03 Confidence activities: speaking tasks, creative work, leadership roles, house activities, and stage participation improve expression and communication.
04 Parent-school communication: regular contact with parents supports discipline, attendance, homework, and consistent student improvement throughout the year.
