Facilities

Spaces for learning, discipline, and participation.

Our facilities support classroom learning, activities, games, events, and confident everyday school life.

What We Offer

Everything students need to learn, play, and grow.

📚

Modern Classrooms

Our classrooms are bright, well-ventilated, and systematically arranged to create a focused learning environment. Every classroom is equipped with a writing board, proper seating, and teaching aids that support board explanation, oral practice, and group activities.

  • Concept clarity with step-by-step board teaching
  • Daily notebook checking and handwriting practice
  • Individual attention and oral questioning sessions
  • Reading aloud and structured revision routines
  • Class tests, worksheets, and weekly assessments
💻

Computer Lab

The school has a dedicated computer lab where students from an early age learn basic computer operations, typing, internet awareness, and educational software. Technology education is delivered in a structured, age-appropriate manner under faculty supervision.

  • Hands-on practice with computers from junior classes
  • Typing, MS Office basics, and educational tools
  • Internet awareness and digital responsibility
  • Computer science as part of the regular timetable
  • Builds confidence for modern study and careers
🏅

Sports Ground & Physical Education

Physical fitness and teamwork are an integral part of school life at Prabha Gyan Jyoti. The spacious sports ground is used daily for PT sessions, games, races, drills, and inter-house competitions that build energy, discipline, and sportsmanship in every student.

  • Daily PT and morning drill for all classes
  • Outdoor games: cricket, kabaddi, kho-kho, athletics
  • Annual Sports Day with inter-house competitions
  • Focus on teamwork, fitness, and healthy competition
  • Yoga and stretching exercises for body awareness
🎤

Assembly & School Events

Morning assembly is a daily tradition that sets a positive tone for the school day. Students participate in prayer, national anthem, news reading, thought of the day, and announcements. Regular events such as Independence Day, Republic Day, farewell, and achievement ceremonies keep school life vibrant and purposeful.

  • Daily morning assembly with prayer and national anthem
  • House system for leadership and healthy competition
  • Republic Day and Independence Day celebrations
  • Annual achievement and merit ceremony for toppers
  • Farewell, cultural events, and inter-class activities
📖

Library & Reading Culture

Reading is encouraged as a daily habit at school. Students have access to age-appropriate books, story collections, and educational reference materials. The school promotes a reading culture through class-wise reading sessions, comprehension practice, and storytelling activities.

  • Collection of story books, textbooks, and references
  • Class-wise reading sessions and comprehension practice
  • Storytelling and oral narration activities
  • Encouragement of reading habits from pre-primary level
  • Monthly reading challenges and book discussions
🎨

Arts, Crafts & Co-Curricular

Beyond academics, students are encouraged to explore their creative side through drawing, craft, music, and cultural activities. Co-curricular activities develop self-expression, confidence, teamwork, and the ability to present oneself in front of an audience.

  • Drawing and craft sessions for all classes
  • Rangoli, poster-making, and seasonal art activities
  • Fancy dress, role-play, and elocution competitions
  • Dance and cultural performances at school events
  • Science exhibitions and project-based learning
